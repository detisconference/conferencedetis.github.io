Call For Paper

International Conference on Demystifyinig Emerging Trends in Intelligent Systems

DETIS-25 is committed to advancing excellence in the domain of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence. Organized by the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology, Gorakhpur, this hybrid-mode international conference (both online and on-site) aims to provide a vibrant platform for academic and industry professionals to explore, discuss, and shape the future of intelligent systems.

With the rapid evolution of intelligent technologies, the volume and complexity of data have reached unprecedented levels. DETIS-25 embraces this transformation by welcoming groundbreaking research and innovations that redefine how we interact with data, extract insights, and develop intelligent solutions. This conference is a convergence point for students, scholars, researchers, and industry practitioners, fostering a collaborative environment for the exchange of knowledge, methodologies, and visionary perspectives.

DETIS-25 brings together thought leaders to deliberate on emerging trends and strategic directions in AI and data science. Participants will gain exposure to cutting-edge technologies, best practices, and future challenges in these domains. The event encourages global collaboration and provides a fertile ground for initiating joint ventures and long-term research partnerships.

Key benefits of attending DETIS-25 include staying informed about the latest scientific breakthroughs, networking with domain experts, and contributing to the sustainable advancement of intelligent systems. Whether you're an academic, researcher, or industry expert, DETIS-25 offers a unique opportunity to be at the forefront of technological transformation.
