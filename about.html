About Us

The International Conference on Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (DETIS) will be organized in a hybrid mode—both online and on-site—at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, India.

In the era of intelligent systems, data-driven innovations are transforming every aspect of technology and society. With the rapid growth in data volume, complexity, and applications, the convergence of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science has become central to modern computational advancements. DETIS aims to bring together undergraduate and postgraduate students, research scholars, academicians, and industry professionals to exchange their latest findings, ideas, and innovations in these evolving domains.

The conference provides a dynamic platform for knowledge sharing, academic networking, and collaborative research. It encourages the dissemination of high-quality research addressing contemporary challenges in intelligent systems, data analytics, machine learning, and their real-world applications. DETIS is committed to promoting interdisciplinary dialogue and fostering partnerships that drive the future of data-centric technologies.
